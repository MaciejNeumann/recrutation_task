How can I become a member?

If you’re a free trial user, a Dynatrace SaaS user, or a Dynatrace employee, you get access to Community by default and can access all forums immediately.

If you’re a Dynatrace Managed user, the access to Community depends on the settings on the Managed cluster.
You can get access either automatically upon your first login to the Managed cluster or the invite must be sent manually by a cluster administrator.
